A few months ago, Robbert became a dad for the second time!

Frank and Robbert created a special edition of a pointy-head crinkle puppet in traditional R&F colors, yellow and red, to connect with the new part of life that they and many of their friends are experiencing as parents.

Pointy-head puppets are like blank canvases that reflect every possible human. When you interact with them, you see yourself in the R&F F&R world, filled with symbols, folklore, rituals, and popular culture. This makes the art come alive and allows everyone to tell their own stories.

Playing with art is fun for adults and kids alike. So do yourself, or a loved one, a favor and buy this unique handmade edition as a (Christmas) gift.
