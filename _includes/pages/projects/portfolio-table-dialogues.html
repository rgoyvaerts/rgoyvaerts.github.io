Table Dialogues

A collection of five intimate, immersive participatory performances.Table Dialogues is a project that unites extended reality, interactive theatre and visual art. It is the brainchild of artist duo Robbert&Frank Frank&Robbert and is based on their personal experience in playing and making. Step into their shoes and experience in small groups – of friends or strangers – what it’s like to enter and activate a new universe.
