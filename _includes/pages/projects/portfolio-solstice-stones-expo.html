FRANK&ROBBERT ROBBERT&FRANK return for the third time to the gallery in Antwerp, where they present new ceramic work that builds on the Neolithic tradition of menhirs, dolmens and symbolic stones. They create contemporary interpretations of ancient themes, activating their creations with ritual acts and participatory performances. The “leftovers” from these interventions become part of the works.

In this video, the artists give a guided tour of the exhibition, explaining each work and delving deeper into the history and personal significance of the sculptures or drawings in question. Prehistory, Freud, dreams, geography, fairy tales, explosions, and alter egos come together in this extensive exposé.
